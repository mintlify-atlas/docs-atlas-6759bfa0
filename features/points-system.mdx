## How to Earn Points

There are multiple ways to accumulate points in NearYou:

### Visit New Places

The primary way to earn points is by visiting new locations you've discovered through the app.

- **First Visit**: Earn points when you visit a place for the first time
- **Check-in**: Use the in-app check-in feature to confirm your visit
- **Location Verification**: Points are awarded when the app verifies you're at the location

<Note>
Points are awarded automatically when you arrive at a discovered place. Make sure location services are enabled for accurate check-ins.
</Note>

### Exploration Milestones

Reach specific milestones to earn bonus points:

- Visit your 5th, 10th, 25th, 50th, and 100th unique place
- Explore all places in a specific category
- Visit places in different neighborhoods or areas
- Complete weekly or monthly exploration challenges

### Special Activities

<Info>
These features are planned for future releases and will expand how you can earn points.
</Info>

- **Review Places**: Share your experience and help other explorers
- **Photo Submissions**: Upload photos of places you visit
- **Referrals**: Invite friends to join NearYou
- **Streak Bonuses**: Earn extra points for exploring multiple days in a row

## Points Structure

Points are awarded based on different actions:

| Action | Points | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| First place visit | 100 | Bonus for discovering a new place |
| Return visit | 25 | Smaller reward for revisiting |
| Category completion | 500 | Complete all places in a category |
| Weekly streak (7 days) | 200 | Visit at least one place each day |
| Achievement unlock | Varies | Based on achievement difficulty |

## Explorer Levels

As you accumulate points, you'll progress through explorer levels:

1. **Novice Explorer** (0-499 points)
2. **Local Guide** (500-1,999 points)
3. **City Navigator** (2,000-4,999 points)
4. **Regional Expert** (5,000-9,999 points)
5. **Master Explorer** (10,000+ points)

<Note>
Levels unlock cosmetic rewards and special features like exclusive place recommendations and early access to new locations.
</Note>

## Tracking Your Progress

View your exploration statistics in your profile:

- Total points earned
- Current explorer level
- Places visited (total and by category)
- Achievements unlocked
- Current streaks
- Personal records and milestones
