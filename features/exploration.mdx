## Navigation & Interface

NearYou features an intuitive tab-based navigation built with Expo Router:

### Home Tab

Your exploration dashboard featuring:

- **Nearby Places**: Discover locations close to your current position
- **Quick Stats**: See your points, level, and recent achievements at a glance
- **Daily Suggestions**: Get personalized place recommendations
- **Active Challenges**: View and track your current exploration goals

### Explore Tab

Dive deeper into exploration with:

- **Interactive Map**: Browse all discovered and undiscovered places
- **Category Filters**: Find specific types of places (coming soon)
- **Distance Controls**: Adjust your exploration radius
- **Search Function**: Look for specific places by name or type

<Note>
The app uses file-based routing with Expo Router, providing smooth navigation and deep linking support across iOS, Android, and web platforms.
</Note>

## Check-In System

When you visit a discovered place, use the check-in feature to:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Automatic Detection">
    The app detects when you're near a discovered place and prompts you to check in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Your Visit">
    Tap the check-in button to confirm you're at the location. The app verifies your position to ensure accurate tracking.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Earn Your Reward">
    Receive points immediately upon successful check-in. First-time visits earn bonus points!
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track Your History">
    Your visit is recorded in your exploration history, contributing to achievements and statistics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Interactive Features

### Haptic Feedback

NearYou uses haptic feedback to create a more engaging experience:

- Subtle vibrations when discovering a new place
- Tactile confirmation when checking in
- Feedback when earning points or unlocking achievements
- Enhanced tab navigation with haptic touch

```typescript
import * as Haptics from 'expo-haptics';

// Example: Haptic feedback on check-in
await Haptics.notificationAsync(
  Haptics.NotificationFeedbackType.Success
);
```

### Smooth Animations

Built with React Native Reanimated for fluid interactions:

- Parallax scroll effects on content pages
- Smooth transitions between screens
- Animated map markers and overlays
- Gesture-based interactions

<Info>
NearYou uses `react-native-reanimated` and `react-native-gesture-handler` to deliver 60fps animations and natural gesture recognition.
</Info>

## Theming & Customization

Explore in style with adaptive theming:

### Light & Dark Mode

The app automatically adapts to your device's appearance settings:

- **Light Mode**: Clean, bright interface perfect for daytime exploration
- **Dark Mode**: Eye-friendly dark theme for evening adventures
- **Automatic Switching**: Seamlessly transitions based on system preferences

### Themed Components

All UI elements use theme-aware components:

- `ThemedView`: Automatically adjusts background colors
- `ThemedText`: Dynamic text colors for optimal readability
- Custom color schemes for maps and overlays


## Offline Support

Explore even without connectivity:

- Previously discovered places remain accessible offline
- Check-ins are queued and synced when connection is restored
- Map data is cached for recently viewed areas
- Your exploration history is stored locally

<Warning>
Active place discovery requires an internet connection to fetch new locations and verify check-ins.
</Warning>

## Privacy & Data

Your exploration data is yours:

- All exploration history is stored locally on your device
- Location data is only used for place discovery and check-ins
- No tracking when the app is closed
- Export your data at any time from settings
- Delete your exploration history whenever you want
